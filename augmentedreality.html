AUGMENTED REALITY

Augmented reality is a technology that "superimposes a computer-generated image on a user's view of the real world, thus providing a composite view". Augmented reality used to be unthinkable, but now its very real and can be used for many things such as Education, Entertainment, Jobs/Industry and many more. Augmented reality has alot of potential and may just be the new rising industry.
